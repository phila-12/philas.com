Digital marketing encompasses all promotional efforts that utilize electronic devices or the internet. It leverages various digital channels such as search engines, social media platforms, email newsletters, and websites to connect with current and potential customers. Key strategies include search engine optimization (SEO), content marketing, social media marketing, pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, email marketing, and analytics-driven strategies. Effective digital marketing aims to reach target audiences, enhance brand awareness, drive website traffic, generate leads, and ultimately, increase sales and revenue for businesses.
